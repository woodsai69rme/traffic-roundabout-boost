# Roundabout WebTraffic — Funding Pitch

## The Problem

Social media creators and brands manage an average of 5–8 platforms simultaneously. Existing tools are fragmented, expensive, and lack AI-powered insights. Small creators and SMBs are priced out of enterprise solutions like Sprout Social ($249/mo) and Hootsuite ($99/mo+).

## The Solution

**Roundabout WebTraffic** is an all-in-one social media management platform that combines:
- **AI-powered content generation & optimization** — leveraging LLMs to write, schedule, and A/B test posts
- **Cross-platform analytics** — unified dashboard for Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Reddit, Snapchat, Twitch
- **Reciprocal engagement communities** — a unique growth engine where creators support each other
- **Content scheduling & automation** — bulk CSV upload, RSS-to-social, evergreen queues
- **Monetization tools** — built-in affiliate tracking, brand deal marketplace, link-in-bio builder
