
# Roundabout WebTraffic - Product Requirements Document

## 1. Overview

Roundabout WebTraffic is a next-generation social media growth and monetization platform designed to help content creators increase their visibility, engagement, and revenue across multiple social media platforms. The platform leverages a reciprocal engagement system, AI-powered insights, and comprehensive analytics to accelerate social media growth and maximize monetization opportunities.

## 2. Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Content Creators**: Individuals producing content on social media platforms seeking to grow their audience
- **Influencers**: Established creators looking to expand their reach and monetize their content more effectively
- **Small Businesses**: Organizations using social media for marketing and customer engagement

### Secondary Users
- **Marketers**: Professionals managing social media accounts for clients
- **Agencies**: Marketing and PR agencies handling multiple client accounts
- **Community Managers**: Professionals building and managing online communities

## 3. User Personas

### Alex - Content Creator (25-34)
- **Goals**: Grow audience across platforms, create consistent content, monetize their following
- **Pain Points**: Managing multiple platforms, finding time to create quality content, understanding analytics
- **Use Case**: Uses the platform to schedule content, generate ideas, and track growth metrics

### Maya - Small Business Owner (35-44)
- **Goals**: Increase brand awareness, engage with customers, drive sales through social
- **Pain Points**: Limited resources, understanding which strategies work, ROI tracking
- **Use Case**: Uses audience insights to target content, AI to speed up content creation, analytics to justify efforts

### Jordan - Marketing Agency Owner (30-40)
- **Goals**: Efficiently manage multiple client accounts, demonstrate value to clients
- **Pain Points**: Scaling operations, reporting to clients, optimizing campaigns
- **Use Case**: Uses team collaboration features, white-label reports, and cross-platform analytics

## 4. Key Product Goals

1. **Accelerate User Growth**: Help users grow their social media following 2-3x faster than traditional methods
2. **Maximize Engagement**: Increase engagement rates by 30%+ through optimized content and timing
3. **Streamline Workflow**: Reduce time spent on content creation and management by 50%
4. **Enhance Monetization**: Help users increase revenue from social media by at least 40%
5. **Provide Actionable Insights**: Convert complex data into clear, actionable recommendations
