
# Product Roadmap

## Overview

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for the Roundabout social media management platform. It provides a timeline of planned features, enhancements, and strategic initiatives to guide development priorities and communicate our vision to stakeholders.

## Strategic Priorities

Our roadmap is guided by the following strategic priorities:

1. **Enhanced AI Integration**: Leverage artificial intelligence to automate content creation, optimization, and performance analysis
2. **Cross-Platform Support**: Expand platform coverage to include emerging and specialized social networks
3. **Enterprise Scalability**: Build features that support large teams, agencies, and enterprise users
4. **Data-Driven Insights**: Develop more powerful analytics and reporting capabilities
5. **Seamless Collaboration**: Enhance team workflows and approval processes

## Feature Request Process

We continuously evaluate and prioritize feature requests from our users and stakeholders. The process for feature consideration includes:

1. **Submission**: Features can be submitted through:
   - In-app feedback
   - Customer support tickets
   - Account management channels
   - User research sessions

2. **Evaluation**: Features are evaluated based on:
   - Strategic alignment
   - User demand (frequency of requests)
   - Technical feasibility
   - Business impact
   - Resource requirements

3. **Prioritization**: Product management team prioritizes features quarterly, considering:
   - Business objectives
   - Market trends
   - Competitive landscape
   - Technical dependencies
   - Resource availability

4. **Communication**: Approved features are added to the roadmap and communicated through:
   - Product update announcements
   - Release notes
   - Customer newsletters
   - Account management channels
