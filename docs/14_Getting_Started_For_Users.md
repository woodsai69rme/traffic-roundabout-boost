# 14. Getting Started for Users

## Welcome to Roundabout WebTraffic!
Your all-in-one social media management platform.

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit the app and click **Get Started**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your email and click the verification link
4. Log in with your credentials

### Step 2: Connect Your Social Accounts
1. Navigate to **Platforms** from the sidebar
2. Click **Connect** on any platform (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)
3. Enter your API credentials or authorize via OAuth
4. You'll see a green "Connected" badge when successful

### Step 3: Schedule Your First Post
1. Go to **Content Planner**
2. Click **New Post**
3. Write your content or use **AI Content Creator** for suggestions
4. Select target platforms
5. Choose a date and time
6. Click **Schedule**

### Step 4: Monitor Performance
1. Visit **Analytics** to see engagement metrics
2. Check **Audience Insights** for demographic data
3. Review best posting times and content performance

## Key Features
- 📅 **Content Calendar** — Visual scheduling across platforms
- 🤖 **AI Content Generator** — Smart content suggestions
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** — Real-time engagement tracking
- 👥 **Audience Insights** — Demographics and behavior patterns
- 🔗 **API Integrations** — Webhooks and data export/import
- 💰 **Monetization Tools** — Revenue tracking and strategies
- 🏘️ **Communities** — Engage with creator communities
