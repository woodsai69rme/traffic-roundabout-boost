# Roundabout WebTraffic — Funding Strategy

## Current Stage

**Pre-seed / Bootstrapped MVP** — The product is built, documented, and ready for beta launch. No external funding has been raised.

## Funding Options Evaluated

### Option 1: Continue Bootstrapping
- **Pros:** Full ownership, no dilution, lean operation
- **Cons:** Slower growth, limited marketing budget, founder burnout risk
- **Best for:** If organic growth exceeds expectations (>500 users in 3 months)

### Option 2: Angel Round (AUD $100K–$250K)
- **Pros:** Smaller dilution (5–10%), smart money from industry angels
- **Cons:** Limited runway (6–9 months), may need to raise again quickly
- **Best for:** Validating PMF before larger raise

### Option 3: Seed Round (AUD $500K) ← **Recommended**
- **Pros:** 18-month runway, hire 2 engineers + growth lead, aggressive marketing
- **Cons:** 15–25% dilution
- **Best for:** Rapid growth to 5,000+ users and $50K+ MRR

### Option 4: VC Series A (AUD $2M–$5M)
- **Pros:** Massive scale potential
- **Cons:** Premature without proven PMF, high dilution
- **Best for:** After reaching $500K+ ARR

## Recommended Strategy

### Phase 1: Bootstrap to Beta (Now → Q2 2026)
- Launch beta with existing MVP
- Target 500 free users, 50 paid users
- Cost: $0 (existing infrastructure)

### Phase 2: Angel Round (Q3 2026)
- Raise AUD $150K from 2–3 angels
- Hire 1 part-time developer
- Target: 1,000 users, $10K MRR
- Dilution: 5–8%

### Phase 3: Seed Round (Q1 2027)
- Raise AUD $500K from seed fund + angels
- Hire 2 full-time engineers + 1 growth marketer
- Target: 5,000 users, $50K MRR
- Dilution: 15–20%

### Phase 4: Series A (Q1 2028)
- Raise AUD $2M–$3M
- Scale to 20,000+ users, $200K+ MRR
- International expansion (US, UK, EU)
- Dilution: 15–25%
